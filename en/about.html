About Educa4Good

A library of learning activities built with a teacher's eye and an editor's care.

Educa4Good was born from a simple observation: preparing good activities takes time — and materials that are beautiful, organized and pedagogically honest are rarer than they should be.

Behind the project there is an unusual level of craft: every page is typeset in LaTeX, the same typographic system used for books and scientific publishing. That is why spacing is consistent, letterforms are the right size for each age, and pages print cleanly even in black and white.

Activities are organized by skill, aligned where possible with Brazil's national curriculum (BNCC), and produced as a living library: new mechanics and themes join the catalog continuously.

The name says it all: education for good. Accessible materials, made with care, for those who teach and those who learn.
