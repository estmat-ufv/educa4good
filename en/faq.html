Frequently asked questions

What do I receive after purchase?
You receive the activity collection as PDFs, organized by category, with the matching answer keys, as described in the offer at the time of purchase.
Are the activities digital or physical?
They are digital PDF files in A4 format, made for you to print wherever you prefer — at home, at school or at a print shop.
Can I print as many times as I want?
Yes. The license allows you to print as many copies as you need for use with your own class or family, under the terms of use.
Can I use them with my students?
Absolutely — the activities were designed for the classroom. Teachers may print for their own classes.
Can I share the files?
No. The PDFs are licensed to the buyer. Sharing, reselling or posting the files online is not allowed — that is what keeps the project alive.
What ages are they for?
The focus is preschool through the early elementary years — generally ages 4 to 8, with age guidance on the activities.
Do I need to install any software?
No. Any PDF reader will do, and one is already installed on most computers and phones.
Do the activities work in black and white?
Yes. Every layout is designed to stay beautiful and legible when printed in black and white.
Are there activities in other languages?
The current catalog is in Portuguese. English and Spanish editions are in preparation — the activities were architected with that in mind.
Will new activities be added?
Yes, the library grows continuously. How you access updates depends on the plan available at the time of purchase.
